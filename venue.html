Find Lake Dorr Cabin:

Driving Directions:
Lake Dorr Cabin is at the southern end of Lake Dorr.
Follow SR 19 north from Altoona.
Turn right on East Altoona Road,
left onto Big Oak Rd.,
then right onto McCall (CR 8378) to reach the cabin.
You can follow small brown and white signs from there.
If you type “Lake Dorr Cabin” into Google Maps, the directions will also guide you to the cabin.
